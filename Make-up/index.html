About Us

Crafting the Future
of Beauty

CM Tech is a specialized cosmetic raw material manufacturer headquartered in Korea, providing innovative ingredients for the global beauty industry.

With dual manufacturing facilities in Pyeongtaek, Korea and Shanghai, China, we deliver high-quality silicone compounds, surface-treated powders, and pigment dispersions to leading cosmetic brands worldwide.

Our vertically integrated production capabilities allow precise control over quality, consistency, and customization — ensuring each ingredient meets the exacting standards of modern cosmetic formulation.
